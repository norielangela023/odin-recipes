Sinigang (fish)

Sinigang was always a recipe that my mom had so much of growing up. This was the major go-to dish that we would almost expect at dinner times

  • Time: 45minutes-1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Salmon fish 2 lbs
cooking oil 2 tbsp
Onion 1 bulb
Garlic 4 cloves
Black peppercorn to tasting
Salt to tasting
tomato 1 tomato (or package of cherry tomatoes)
spinach 1 lbs spincach leaves
Water 6 cups
boulon 2 cubes/packets (water and boulon can be substitued with seafood broth)
Celery 1 pack of celery hearts
ocra 1/2 lbs

Directions

Step 1: Chop up the Onions, garlic, celery, tomatotes, and fish to suitable sizes for cooking.

Step 2: in a pot, warm the cooking oil over medium heat and saute the galic and onions until onions are clear

Step 3: cook the fish in in the pot and cook for 3 minutes

Step 4: take out the fish and pour the water and boulon (or cooking broth) and warm until simmering

Step 5: Add celery and cook for 20 minutes

Step 6: Add the fish and ocra and cook for another 5 mintues.

Step 7: Add spinach and tomatotes and cook for an additional 5 minutes

Step 8: Add salt and pepper to taste.

Step 9: Serve warm with rice and enjoy!
