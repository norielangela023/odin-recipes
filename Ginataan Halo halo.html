Ginataan Halo Halo

I didn't get to eat this often as it was always a desert that was reserved for parties. But, when we did have it I would alwasy grab a bowl of this desert and enjoy it to the last drop.

  • Time: 3 hours
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
coconut milk 2 cans (400 mL cans)
Tapioca (or sago) pearls 1 cup (already cooked)
Jackfruit 1 cup (make sure they are cut into strips)
Glutinous rice flour 2 1lbs bags
Ube flour/paste (or ube flavoring) 1/2 lbs(2tbsp)
plantain bananas 4 bananas cubed
sweet potatos 2 potatoes cubed
sugar 3/4 cups
water 4 cups

Directions

Step 1: Add 1 cup of water to the glutinous rice flour and mix. divide in half and mix one half with the ube flavoring. Separate and roll into 1 in diameter balls and set aside.

Step 2: Combined the remaing water with the coconut milk and surgar in a pot and bring to a boil.

Step 3: Add the sweet potatoes and banana and cook for roughtly 3-6 minutes

Step 4: Add the sticky rice balls and let simmer until they float.

Step 5: Add the jackfruit and cooked tapioca pearls and let simmer for 3-5 minutes minimum.

Step 6: Once the broth has thickened (it should be gooey) you can serve. Add water if it becomes too thick. This can be served hot or cold.
