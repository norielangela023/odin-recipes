Fried Rice

Friend rice was mostly a staple in the house with many of the add ins being leftovers

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
>
Cooked white rice 5 cups
cooking oil 2 tbsp
Peas 1 cup (can be frozen)
carrots 1/2 inch cubes
Soy Sauce 4 cups +extra as needed
Onion 1 bulb
Garlic 4 cloves
Black peppercorn to tasting
Salt to tasting
Meat (of your choosing must be cooked) 1 cup
extra ingredients extra ingredients can be added to your liking

Directions

Step 1: Chop up the Onions, chicken, and garlic to suitable sizes for cooking.

Step 2: lightly salt the chicken and set aside.

Step 3: warm the cooking oil over medium heat and saute the galic and onions until onions are clear

Step 4: Warm the Stove to medium heat and saute the Garlic and rest of the onions until they clear.

Step 5: Carefully put the chicken in the pan and cook for 5 minutes

Step 6: Add the rest of the ingredients and mix for a minimum of 5 extra minutes to ensure everything is mixed and cooked through. (add salt and peper to taste).

Step 7: remove from heat and serve warm.
