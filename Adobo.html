Adobo

Considered the national dish of the Philippines, this recepie is a mix of my family's recipe and of what I've added over the years. No week was complete without this dish appearing at dinner at least once.

  • Time: 30 minutes (does not include prep time)
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 4 lbs
cooking oil 2 tbsp
Soy Sauce 4 cups +extra as needed
vinegar 1/2 cup
Soy Sauce 4 cups +extra as needed
Toyomansi 4 tbsp (lime can be a subsitute)
Bay leaves 4-5 whole leaves (optional)
Onion 1 bulb
Garlic 5 cloves
Black peppercorn 3 tbsp

Directions

Step 1: Chop up the Onions and place 1/2 in a bowl with the Black peppercorns, Bay leaves, Vinegar, and Soy sauce.

Step 2: Cut the chicken into 1 inch cubes and place in the bowl.

Step 3: Cover the bowl and place in the fridge for a minimum of 30 minutes prior to cooking.

Step 4: Warm the Stove to medium heat and saute the Garlic and rest of the onions until they clear.

Step 5: Carefully pour the contents of the bowl in the pan and saute until the chicken is cooked through.

Step 6: Serve over white rice. use the remaining liquid as a pour over sauce for the dish.
