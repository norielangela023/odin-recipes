Filipino Food recipies

FFilipino food has been a part of my life since I could remember. Home-cooked meals and family time was one of the most important things in my childhood. These are a few of the ones that are integrated in my memories that always bring a smile to my face. Hopefully with this project I will be able to capture some of the recipies I had with a few extra changes of my own so that this can be shared with others to try.
